FAQ

Is pricing transparent?

Yes. Setup, monthly retainers and pass-through costs are separated clearly.

Is there a long contract?

No long lock-in for sprint work. Retainers are month-to-month unless a separate agreement says otherwise.

What happens after 12 months?

You can renew, reduce scope, or take the documented system in-house.

Can I cancel?

Yes, with notice as agreed in the proposal. Completed sprint work remains yours.

How is this different from WATI or AiSensy?

Those are tools. AICS designs the enquiry process, copy, pages, consent flow, reporting and operating rhythm around the tools.

What if there are no results?

Founder customers get a money-back guarantee under the published terms if there is no measurable lift in the defined review period.

How do GST and billing work?

Billing details and taxes are finalized in the proposal/invoice based on the business setup at the time of purchase.
