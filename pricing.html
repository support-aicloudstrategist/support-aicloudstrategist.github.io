Yes. You own your website content, lead data and approved documents.

How is DPDP compliance different from what my CA or lawyer handles?

Your CA or lawyer handles legal interpretation. AICS helps operationalize consent and data handling.

What if my business doesn't see measurable lift in 60 days?

Founder Program customers get a 100% money-back guarantee if there is no measurable lift in 60 days.

Do I need to register a Pvt Ltd or LLP to work with you?

No. Clinics, labs, proprietors, firms and companies can work with us.

How long does each package take to deliver?

DPDP Sprint takes about 2 weeks. Lead Recovery Desk takes about 4 weeks.

Can I upgrade from Launch Desk to Lead Recovery Desk later?

Yes. Launch Desk is designed as the entry point.
