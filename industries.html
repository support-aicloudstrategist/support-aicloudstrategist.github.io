Common questions

Do I need a CRM?

No. We can start with Google Sheets/CRM-lite and upgrade later only if useful.

Can this work with WhatsApp?

Yes. The first design assumption is WhatsApp-first, because most Indian SMB enquiries land there.

Will AI replace my staff?

No. The goal is to reduce repeated work, missed follow-ups and unclear ownership.

How fast can we launch?

A focused first sprint can usually launch the first useful flow in days, depending on access and scope.
