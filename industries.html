Industries

Every sector has a different leakage pattern.

AICS packages should speak in the language of the business owner, not generic IT terms.

Clinics & Aesthetic Practices

Missed calls, appointment gaps, WhatsApp chaos, weak review follow-up.

System:Callback queue, appointment reminder, website service page, privacy-safe enquiry flow.

Diagnostics & Labs

Enquiries scattered across phone/email/WhatsApp; trust and location clarity matter.

System:Lead capture, test enquiry workflow, owner dashboard, no patient data request.

Manufacturing & Workshops

Weak catalogue visibility, vendor follow-up, manual reporting and quote leakage.

System:Catalogue site, enquiry tracker, supplier reminders, production/status dashboard.

Education & Coaching

Admission leads go cold across calls, WhatsApp and forms.

System:Counselling flow, fee reminders, parent/student updates and lead scoring.

Real Estate

Unqualified leads, missed site visits and slow broker follow-up.

System:Lead qualification, visit booking, project enquiry forms and follow-up reminders.

CA, Legal & Professional Firms

Slow intake, document chasing, deadline risk and weak trust presentation.

System:Client intake, document checklist, deadline reminders and service pages.

Salons & Wellness

Repeat visits, offers, appointments and reviews are not tracked systematically.

System:Booking CTA, reminder flow, review request, offer/newsletter workflow.

Food, Retail & Local Brands

Orders, enquiries, campaigns and repeat customer nudges need structure.

System:WhatsApp ordering/enquiry path, promotions, customer follow-up and Google visibility.
