About AICloudStrategist

Done-for-you AI lead recovery and DPDP readiness for Indian clinics, labs and local service businesses.

Who we are

AICloudStrategist is an India-first consulting and execution studio for clinics, diagnostic labs, local service businesses, and practical SMB owners who need technology to bring more enquiries, not more confusion. We build done-for-you lead recovery systems: better websites, clear enquiry forms, WhatsApp follow-up, simple automation, and DPDP-ready privacy basics. The goal is straightforward: help a real business capture every serious enquiry, respond faster, keep records clean, and look more trustworthy online without needing an internal IT team.

AICloudStrategist is led by Rajiv, a Lead Architect with 22+ years across IT, Unix/Linux, datacenter operations, AWS, Azure, GCP, FinOps, DevOps, SRE, observability, cybersecurity, and AI/MLOps. That background matters because small businesses do not need a decorative website alone; they need secure, reliable systems that can scale without creating daily operational trouble. Rajiv brings senior architecture judgment to every engagement, from privacy flows to automation design. Connect on LinkedIn.

How we work

Rajiv leads every project personally; AI agents handle research, drafting, checking, formatting, and repeatable execution under approval gates so we deliver faster and more affordably than a traditional agency. We do not ask a shop owner or clinic manager to learn tools first. We study the current business, write the simple plan, build the assets, test the handoff, and keep the owner involved only where a real decision or approval is needed.

We are early-stage, so we are direct about what is proven, what is being built, and where founder customers can shape the service. We do not publish fake testimonials, borrowed client logos, or inflated metrics. Where a package includes a money-back guarantee, we explain the conditions clearly before work begins. Our promise is to keep the scope practical, pricing transparent, communication plain, and every deliverable useful enough for a real business owner to understand and operate.
