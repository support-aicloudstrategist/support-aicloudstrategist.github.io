## Reverification checklist before prospect-level approval

For each target, verify and record:

1. **Official entity and location** — exact legal/brand name, city/location, whether it is independent, chain-owned, franchise, trust-run, or hospital group.
2. **Public source freshness** — official website live, Google Business profile, LinkedIn/company page if available, recent activity or current service page.
3. **Contact route** — official public enquiry email/form/phone/WhatsApp only; no scraped personal numbers; no patient-care emergency channels.
4. **Decision role** — founder, owner, administrator, operations head, centre manager, IT/digital head, or business development head.
5. **Trigger evidence** — visible reason for scorecard relevance, e.g. online booking, reports, packages, multi-location operations, home collection, old website, weak CTA, missing dashboard language, or complex patient flow.
6. **Exclusion check** — avoid contacting emergency numbers, doctors’ personal clinical numbers, patient support hotlines for sales, or organisations with explicit vendor/contact restrictions.
7. **Consent/compliance note** — outreach must be one concise B2B message, identify AICloudStrategist clearly, include no medical claims, and stop after opt-out/non-response per Raj’s rules.
