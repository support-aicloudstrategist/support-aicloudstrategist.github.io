## Production-ready website copy

### Healthcare Digital Readiness Scorecard

#### For clinics, diagnostic centres, pathology labs, small hospitals, and nursing homes

Is your healthcare business losing time, revenue, or patient trust because digital systems are not connected?

AICloudStrategist helps healthcare leadership identify practical gaps across enquiries, appointments, billing, reports, backups, security basics, cloud systems, WhatsApp workflows, and management visibility — without forcing a full system replacement.

The **Healthcare Digital Readiness Scorecard** gives owners, administrators, and hospital leadership a clear view of what is working, what is risky, and what can be improved in the next 30 days.

**Best fit for**

- Specialty clinics and multi-doctor practices
- Diagnostic centres and pathology labs
- Small hospitals and nursing homes
- Healthcare businesses planning HIS, EMR, LIS, cloud, backup, automation, or digital presence upgrades

**What we review**

- Patient enquiry capture from calls, Google Business, website, WhatsApp, referrals, and walk-ins
- Appointment booking, reminders, rescheduling, no-shows, and queue visibility
- Billing, payment, report delivery, prescription delivery, and follow-up leakage
- Backup routines, restore readiness, ransomware resilience, and continuity planning
- Access control, user roles, password hygiene, staff exit process, and privacy basics
- Website trust signals, appointment CTA, doctor/service visibility, and location clarity
- Leadership dashboards for leads, appointments, revenue, reports, and pending actions

**What you receive**

1. **Leadership discovery session** — 30–45 minutes to understand current systems, pain points, patient journey, and priorities.
2. **Digital workflow map** — enquiry → appointment → billing → consultation/test → report/prescription → follow-up.
3. **Risk and leakage scorecard** — practical 1–5 ratings across patient experience, revenue leakage, backup/security, automation, and visibility.
4. **30-day improvement roadmap** — quick fixes, medium-effort improvements, and optional implementation roadmap.
5. **Implementation estimate** — non-binding guidance for website/WhatsApp automation, backup hardening, analytics dashboard, or cloud architecture clean-up.

**Healthcare Digital Readiness Audit:** ₹15,000–₹35,000 + applicable taxes, depending on organisation size and number of systems/locations reviewed.

> This is not a medical, legal, or regulatory certification. It is a practical technology and workflow readiness review for business improvement, operational resilience, and better digital execution.

**Primary CTA button:** Book a 20-minute readiness review  
**Secondary link:** Download the Healthcare Digital Readiness Scorecard
