What we review

We map the practical journey from patient enquiry to appointment, billing, consultation/test, report or prescription delivery, follow-up and leadership visibility.

  • Website, Google, call and WhatsApp enquiry capture

Frequently asked questions

Is this a software replacement project?

No. The first step is a focused readiness audit. If software changes are useful, they are proposed separately after the scorecard and roadmap.

Do you need patient data?

No patient data is needed for the initial review. We prefer process walkthroughs, system names, anonymised examples and screenshots with sensitive details hidden.

Is this a certification?

No. This is not DPDP, NABH, ABDM, legal, medical or cybersecurity certification. It is a practical technology and workflow readiness review.

What will we receive?

A workflow map, 10-area scorecard, practical risk/leakage register, 30-day roadmap and optional implementation estimate.

How long does it take?

Most reviews take 5–8 working days, depending on locations, systems, team availability and clarity of current workflows.

What if we already use HIS, EMR, LIS or billing software?

That is normal. We look for gaps around the software: enquiry capture, handoffs, reminders, report delivery, backups, access hygiene and dashboards.
