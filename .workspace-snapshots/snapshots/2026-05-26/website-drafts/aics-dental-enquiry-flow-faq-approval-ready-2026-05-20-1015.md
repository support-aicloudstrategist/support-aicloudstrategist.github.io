## Approval-ready FAQ copy

**What is the Dental High-Value Enquiry Flow Check?**  
It is a short digital workflow review for dental clinics that want better visibility across implant, aligner, braces, cosmetic-dentistry and consultation enquiries. AICloudStrategist maps how enquiries arrive through calls, WhatsApp, website forms, Google profile links and social channels, then highlights practical follow-up gaps.

**Is this a marketing campaign or a clinical service?**  
No. This is not clinical advice and does not make treatment, medical-outcome or guaranteed-growth claims. It focuses only on digital enquiry handling, missed-call recovery, appointment follow-up discipline, owner visibility and simple workflow improvements.

**Who is it best suited for?**  
It is best suited for dental clinics, orthodontic practices, implant centres, aligner providers and cosmetic dentistry teams where high-intent enquiries may be handled by multiple people across phone, WhatsApp, social DMs and front-desk follow-ups.

**What will the clinic receive after the review?**  
The clinic receives a concise improvement note with 3–5 practical fixes. These may cover clearer call-to-WhatsApp handoff, faster first-response ownership, pending consultation tracking, missed-call follow-up, reminder discipline, or a lightweight owner-visible follow-up board.

**Do we need to share patient records?**  
No patient records are required for the initial review. The review can begin from public-facing enquiry paths, response workflows, owner visibility requirements and non-sensitive operational discussion.

**Can this work with tools the clinic already uses?**  
Usually, yes. The first step is to understand the current process and identify improvements that can start with existing tools where possible. Automation or cloud workflow changes are recommended only when they appear practical and worthwhile.

**How long does the first review take?**  
The starting point is a 20-minute Dental Enquiry Flow Check. If there is a clear fit, AICloudStrategist can then propose a simple next-step roadmap.

**What is the safest next step?**  
Request a 20-minute Dental Enquiry Flow Check and share which enquiry channels matter most today: calls, WhatsApp, website, Google profile, Instagram/Facebook, referrals, or walk-ins.
