Frequently asked questions

Is this a marketing campaign or a clinical service?

No. This is not clinical advice and does not make treatment, medical-outcome or guaranteed-growth claims. It focuses only on digital enquiry handling, missed-call recovery, appointment follow-up discipline, owner visibility and simple workflow improvements.

Who is it best suited for?

Dental clinics, orthodontic practices, implant centres, aligner providers and cosmetic dentistry teams where high-intent enquiries may be handled by multiple people across phone, WhatsApp, social DMs and front-desk follow-ups.

Do we need to share patient records?

No patient records are required for the initial review. The review can begin from public-facing enquiry paths, response workflows, owner visibility requirements and non-sensitive operational discussion.

How long does the first review take?

The starting point is a 20-minute Dental Enquiry Flow Check. If there is a clear fit, AICloudStrategist can then propose a simple next-step roadmap.
