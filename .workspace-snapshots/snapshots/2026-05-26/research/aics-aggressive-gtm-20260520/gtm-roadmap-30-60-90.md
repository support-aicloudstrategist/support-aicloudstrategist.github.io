# AICloudStrategist 30/60/90-Day GTM Roadmap

_Date: 2026-05-20_  
_Context: AICloudStrategist is Rajiv's solo AI-operated cloud + AI consulting venture for Indian SMBs. Vision: help businesses move from no website → digital presence → automation → compliance/security/trust._

## 1. GTM Positioning

### Core Promise
AICloudStrategist helps Indian small and mid-sized businesses become digitally ready, automated, and trustworthy without hiring a full IT team.

### Simple Market Message
> “We help Indian businesses get a professional website, automate daily work, and become compliance/security ready using practical cloud and AI systems.”

### Differentiators
- Cloud + AI + automation + security under one practical consulting umbrella.
- AI-operated delivery model keeps execution lean and affordable.
- Indian SMB-friendly pricing, language, and compliance context.
- Founder has 22+ years of enterprise IT/cloud/FinOps/SRE/security experience.
- Offer ladder allows clients to start small and expand.

## 2. Target Segments

Prioritize segments where digital presence, workflow automation, and trust/compliance directly affect revenue.

### Tier 1: Immediate Targets
1. **Local service businesses**
   - Clinics, coaching institutes, salons, repair services, consultants, accounting firms.
   - Pain: poor web presence, WhatsApp-only operations, missed leads, no structured follow-up.
   - Entry offer: website + lead form + WhatsApp integration.

2. **Small B2B service providers**
   - IT services, HR/recruitment agencies, logistics brokers, architects, CA/legal firms.
   - Pain: unprofessional website, manual proposals, weak lead tracking, weak credibility.
   - Entry offer: website refresh + CRM-lite + proposal/follow-up automation.

3. **Export/import and manufacturing SMBs**
   - Greater Noida/Noida/Delhi NCR industrial units, traders, distributors.
   - Pain: buyer trust, catalogue visibility, enquiry management, compliance/security gaps.
   - Entry offer: digital catalogue site + enquiry workflow + email/domain hygiene.

### Tier 2: Expand After Proof
4. **E-commerce/retail brands**
   - Pain: fragmented tools, manual order/support tracking, weak analytics.
   - Offer: automation + dashboards + customer support workflows.

5. **Healthcare/wellness operators**
   - Pain: appointment workflows, data privacy, reminders, reputation.
   - Offer: booking + reminders + consent/privacy hygiene.

6. **Education/coaching businesses**
   - Pain: lead capture, class reminders, payment follow-up, parent/student communication.
   - Offer: lead funnel + automation + simple LMS/payment integrations.

## 3. Offer Ladder

The ladder should allow low-friction entry and predictable upsell.

### Level 1 — Digital Presence Starter
**For:** Businesses with no website or poor website.  
**Outcome:** Professional online presence and basic lead capture.  
**Typical deliverables:**
- 1-5 page website or landing page.
- Domain/DNS/email hygiene check.
- Google Business Profile guidance/checklist.
- Contact form, WhatsApp CTA, basic analytics.
- Basic SEO metadata and performance hygiene.

**Indicative pricing:** ₹15,000-₹45,000 depending scope.  
**Timeline:** 3-7 days.

### Level 2 — Automation Starter
**For:** Businesses already getting leads but managing manually.  
**Outcome:** Fewer missed enquiries, faster follow-ups, cleaner operations.  
**Typical deliverables:**
- Lead capture → Google Sheet/CRM pipeline.
- WhatsApp/email follow-up templates.
- Appointment/reminder workflows.
- Invoice/proposal/document automation basics.
- Simple dashboard for leads/tasks/status.

**Indicative pricing:** ₹35,000-₹90,000 setup + optional ₹5,000-₹20,000/month support.  
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks.

### Level 3 — Trust, Compliance & Security Hygiene
**For:** Businesses handling customer data, payments, B2B clients, or regulated workflows.  
**Outcome:** Better trust posture and lower operational/security risk.  
**Typical deliverables:**
- Privacy policy, terms, consent-flow checklist.
- Email/domain security: SPF, DKIM, DMARC check.
- Website security headers/basic hardening.
- Backup and access review.
- Data handling SOP for leads/customer data.
- Cloud cost/security posture review if applicable.

**Indicative pricing:** ₹50,000-₹1,50,000 depending depth.  
**Timeline:** 1-3 weeks.

### Level 4 — Managed Cloud + AI Operations
**For:** Growing SMBs needing ongoing systems support.  
**Outcome:** AI-assisted operations, dashboards, monitoring, reliability, and monthly improvements.  
**Typical deliverables:**
- Cloud architecture review and implementation.
- Observability/SRE basics.
- FinOps/cost optimization.
- AI assistants/workflows for support, reporting, admin tasks.
- Monthly automation/security roadmap.

**Indicative pricing:** ₹25,000-₹1,00,000/month retainer.  
**Timeline:** Ongoing.

## 5. Lead Sources

### Free / Low-Cost Sources
- Google Maps / Google Business Profiles.
- LinkedIn company search and founder profiles.
- IndiaMART/TradeIndia/export directories.
- Justdial/Sulekha/public business directories.
- Local chamber of commerce/member directories.
- Startup India/public incubator lists.
- Industry association directories.
- Existing websites found through local search queries.

### Search Patterns
- “clinic Greater Noida contact”
- “manufacturing company Noida website”
- “CA firm Delhi NCR website”
- “coaching institute Noida admission contact”
- “exporter India contact us”
- “B2B service company India contact”

### Lead Scoring
Score each lead 1-5:
- 5 = Strong fit: weak website + clear business + public contact + high trust need.
- 4 = Good fit: some web presence but obvious lead/automation gaps.
- 3 = Possible fit: unclear budget or generic need.
- 2 = Weak fit: very small/low urgency/no public contact.
- 1 = Avoid: irrelevant, spammy, no legitimate business signal.

## 6. Daily Prospecting Motion

### Daily Workflow
1. Select one target segment for the day.
2. Find 15-40 prospects from public sources.
3. Score each prospect.
4. Capture visible pain points.
5. Draft personalized outreach for top 5-15.
6. Queue messages for approval.
7. Send only approved messages.
8. Log outcome and next follow-up date.

### Personalization Framework
Each message should include:
- One specific observation.
- One clear business impact.
- One small offer/review.
- One soft CTA.

Example structure:
> Hi [Name/Team], I noticed [specific website/workflow issue]. For businesses like yours, this usually means [impact]. AICloudStrategist helps with [relevant offer]. I can share a quick 3-point digital readiness review if useful.

## 7. Approval-Safe Outreach System

No external outreach should be sent autonomously without Rajiv’s approval.

### Approval Rules
- AI may research leads and draft messages freely.
- AI must queue outreach drafts with recipient, channel, and exact message.
- Rajiv must approve before first contact.
- Follow-ups also require approval unless Rajiv explicitly pre-approves a specific sequence.
- No mass blasting.
- No scraping behind login walls.
- No contacting personal numbers unless publicly listed for business enquiries and approved.
- No misleading claims, fake case studies, or pretending to have staff.

### Outreach Queue Fields
- Lead ID
- Recipient/business
- Channel
- Source URL
- Exact message draft
- Offer referenced
- Risk flags
- Approval status: Draft / Approved / Rejected / Sent
- Sent date
- Reply status

### Safe Batch Size
- First 30 days: 5-10/day.
- Days 31-60: 10-20/day.
- Days 61-90: 20-30/day only if replies and complaint risk remain healthy.

## 10. CRM Stages

Recommended simple pipeline:
1. **Raw Lead** — Found but not assessed.
2. **Qualified** — Fits segment and has visible need.
3. **Drafted** — Outreach draft created.
4. **Pending Approval** — Waiting for Rajiv approval.
5. **Approved** — Ready to send.
6. **Contacted** — First message sent.
7. **Follow-up Due** — No reply yet, next touch scheduled.
8. **Interested** — Positive response.
9. **Discovery Scheduled** — Call/review booked.
10. **Reviewed** — Digital readiness review completed.
11. **Proposal Sent** — Offer shared.
12. **Won** — Paid project/retainer.
13. **Lost** — Not moving forward.
14. **Do Not Contact** — Must not be contacted again.

## 11. Risk Controls

### Legal/Compliance
- Use only publicly available business contact information.
- Respect opt-outs and do-not-contact requests immediately.
- Avoid spammy bulk campaigns.
- Keep claims truthful and evidence-backed.
- Use clear sender identity.
- Do not imply partnerships/certifications unless real.
- Avoid collecting sensitive personal data unless necessary and consented.

### Brand/Reputation
- Do not shame businesses publicly in teardown content.
- Use anonymized examples unless permission is granted.
- Keep outreach short, useful, and non-pushy.
- Avoid overpromising AI outcomes.
- Do not claim “fully compliant” unless a qualified legal review supports it.

### Delivery Risk
- Use fixed-scope starter packages.
- Document assumptions and exclusions.
- Collect 50% advance for project work where possible.
- Use checklists to prevent missed handover items.
- Prefer managed tools with low operational burden for SMB clients.
- Do not build complex custom systems before validating need.

### Financial Risk
- Do not spend more than Rajiv’s threshold: ₹2,000 / $25 without approval.
- Prioritize free/low-cost lead sources first.
- Avoid paid ads until offer conversion is proven.
- Track time spent per project to validate margins.

### AI/Automation Risk
- Human approval for all outbound messages.
- Review AI-generated legal/compliance copy before publication.
- Keep client credentials out of chat/logs where possible.
- Use least-privilege access for client systems.
- Maintain client-specific delivery notes securely.
