# AICloudStrategist Website Reform Blueprint — Respond.io-Inspired, AICS-Differentiated

Date: 2026-05-20  
Owner: Jarvis subagent  
Scope: Execution-ready website reform plan only. No live website changes, outreach, or public posting.

## 1. Executive direction

AICloudStrategist should borrow Respond.io's maturity pattern — clear category narrative, multi-channel contact, productized plans, proof assets, resources, calculators, AI-agent workflows, and trust-first buying paths — but must **not** become a Respond.io clone.

Respond.io sells conversation management software. AICS sells **done-for-you digital growth and trust infrastructure for Indian SMBs**, with an advisory lane for cloud/security/FinOps/AI readiness. The new website should position AICS as:

> **Your AI-operated digital growth desk for Indian SMBs — website, WhatsApp lead recovery, automation, privacy, cloud and security foundations handled end-to-end.**

Primary buyer message:

> **Stop losing enquiries. Get a professional website, WhatsApp-first lead capture, follow-up automation, and trust/privacy basics — delivered by AICloudStrategist agents under senior cloud/security oversight.**

## 2. What to learn from Respond.io

Public reference patterns observed from Respond.io homepage/pricing:

- Category narrative: customer conversations break across chat, calls, email and CRMs; unified inbox + AI agents solve it.
- Journey framework: Capture → Convert → Retain.
- Strong multi-channel angle: WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook Messenger, email, calls, CRM.
- Pricing ladder: Starter, Growth, Advanced, Enterprise.
- Proof architecture: brand logos, case studies, ROI claims, G2/top-rated trust markers.
- Product depth: AI agents, workflows, broadcasts, lifecycle management, integrations, dashboards, security features.
- Conversion CTAs: Talk to Sales, Start Free Trial, interactive tour, pricing calculator.
- Resources: customers, blogs, help, tutorials, calculators.

AICS adaptation:

- Replace software-only promise with **service + agent-operated implementation**.
- Replace global enterprise logos with **sample deliverables, before/after audits, public checklists, founder expertise, DPIIT recognition if verified, and transparent SOPs**.
- Replace free trial with **Free Business Leakage Review**.
- Replace SaaS MAC pricing with **fixed sprint + managed agent desk plans**.
- Replace Capture/Convert/Retain with **Find → Capture → Recover → Automate → Trust → Scale**.

For businesses that need professional online presence and enquiry capture.

Best for: no website, weak website, local SMB.

For businesses getting enquiries but losing follow-ups.

Indicative price: **from ₹14,999 setup + optional monthly management**

Includes Launch Desk plus:

- Lead tracker/CRM sheet or lightweight CRM
- WhatsApp follow-up templates
- Missed-call/manual enquiry workflow
- Follow-up stages
- Daily/weekly owner dashboard
- Source tracking
- Simple agent routing SOP

Best for: clinics, diagnostics, coaching, local services, factories.

#### 3. **Automation & Trust Desk** — replaces Advanced

For businesses needing serious workflow automation and privacy/trust foundation.

Indicative price: **from ₹25,000 setup**, scoped by workflow count.

Includes Lead Recovery Desk plus:

- Automation workflows
- DPDP/privacy/consent review
- Vendor register/template
- Security hygiene checklist
- Role-based access recommendations
- Dashboard enhancements
- Monthly improvement backlog
- Approved response library

Best for: healthcare, D2C, institutes, service businesses handling personal data.

#### 4. **Managed Growth Desk** — replaces Enterprise

For multi-location, higher-volume or cloud/advisory clients.

Pricing: **custom monthly retainer / scoped advisory**

Includes:

- Multi-channel lead desk governance
- Custom integrations/APIs
- Cloud/security/FinOps review

- Agent-run monitoring and reporting
- Review calls
- SOPs and escalation model
- Multi-location / multi-brand support

Best for: serious growth teams, franchises, SaaS/IT, regulated businesses, cloud-heavy teams.

## 11. Lead-routing automation ownership by agents

### Agent roles

| Agent | Owns | Inputs | Outputs | Escalates when |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Intake Agent | first classification | channel, message, source URL, UTM | lead type + urgency | unclear/sensitive request |
| Qualification Agent | discovery | business type, city, website, problem | recommended tier + questions | pricing exception or high-value lead |
| Follow-up Agent | reminders | lead stage, last touch, SLA | next follow-up task/template | customer objection or complaint |
| Social Agent | FB/IG/Telegram | social DMs/comments | route to WhatsApp/form | public negative/sensitive post |
| Email Agent | inbound email | email body/attachments | CRM row + draft reply | legal/security/client data |
| Sales Agent | proposal path | qualified lead | proposal brief + call agenda | custom enterprise scope |
| Trust Agent | privacy/security | forms/pages/vendor data | trust checklist gaps | legal/compliance uncertainty |
| Reporting Agent | dashboards | CRM/sheets/forms | daily/weekly owner report | dashboard anomaly |

- Homepage clearly communicates AICS as website + lead recovery + automation + trust desk.
- Contact page includes WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger route, Instagram, Telegram plan/route, email and talk-to-sales form.
- Pricing page has four AICS tiers with INR-first clarity.
- At least one product page and two healthcare industry pages are live.
- Forms capture UTM/source/referrer and route to a lead tracker.
- Trust/privacy disclaimers are visible.
- Sitemap/schema updated.
- Mobile CTAs work.
- No unverified proof claims are published.
