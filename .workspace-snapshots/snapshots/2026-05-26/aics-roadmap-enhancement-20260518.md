# AICloudStrategist Roadmap Enhancement Plan — 2026-05-18

## Executive aim

Fast-track AICloudStrategist from “assets and pilots are ready” to a repeatable customer onboarding engine. The roadmap should prioritize paid conversion, rapid first value, approval-controlled outreach, and scalable delivery across two lanes:

1. **AICS Local** — website, digital trust, WhatsApp/enquiry capture, CRM-lite and follow-up automation for Indian SMBs.
2. **AICS Advisory** — cloud cost, architecture, reliability, security, DevSecOps/SRE and AI/MLOps readiness for cloud-heavy businesses.

Operating rule: every roadmap item must connect to one of these outcomes — qualified leads, approved outreach, discovery booked, offer/deposit, onboarding completed, first value delivered, proof/referral captured.

## Current foundation to build on

- Service catalog and 10-day GTM assets already define Local and Advisory lanes.
- Website/RAG/chatbot and calling stack are live or near-live for sales-led qualification.
- Business channels exist: website, WhatsApp Business, voice DID, internal CRM templates.
- CRM SOP already requires approval before outreach and logs leads/interactions/approvals.
- Voice SDR flow has been hardened around short permission-based discovery and audit/demo booking.
- Blueprint already defines the customer journey: **Pain → Offer → Price → Leads → Outreach → Questions → Close → Onboarding → First Value → Proof → Repeat**.

## Roadmap enhancement priorities

### 1. Tighten the offer ladder

| Ladder stage | AICS Local | AICS Advisory | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free / diagnostic | Website/enquiry leakage review; call/WhatsApp audit teaser | Cloud risk/cost hypothesis teaser from public/basic inputs | Create low-friction discovery demand |
| Entry paid | **Digital Trust Audit** ₹14,999–₹24,999 | **Cloud Snapshot Review** ₹49,999–₹75,000 | Convert hesitant buyers quickly |
| Main paid | **Local Growth Sprint** ₹49,999–₹79,999 | **Cloud Advisory Sprint** ₹1.5L–₹3L | Core revenue offer |
| Anchor | **Local Automation Growth System** ₹2.5L–₹5L | **90-Day Cloud Transformation Advisory** ₹7.5L–₹15L | Upsell / retainer / enterprise path |

Recommendation: sell the **main sprint** by default, use entry offers for price resistance, and use anchor offers to make the main sprint feel practical.

### 2. Standardize onboarding and time-to-first-value

Targets:
- Payment/deposit to welcome + intake link: **under 5 minutes**.
- Intake submission to first AI-generated draft: **under 30 minutes**.
- First polished scorecard/roadmap: **within 24 hours**.
- First visible implementation action: **within 72 hours**.

Local first-value pack:
- Digital Presence Scorecard.
- Enquiry Capture Gap Report.
- WhatsApp Follow-Up Flow Draft.
- Trust Page Checklist.
- 30-Day Implementation Roadmap.

Advisory first-value pack:
- Cloud Discovery Scorecard.
- Cost/Risk/Reliability Hypothesis Map.
- Access-light Review Plan.
- Quick-Win Checklist.
- Draft 30/60/90 Roadmap.

### 3. Convert assets into one onboarding system

Needed systems:
1. **Offer + pricing decision sheet** — maps prospect pain to the correct tier and next step.
2. **Onboarding forms** — separate Local and Advisory intake forms using the existing 20-question intake lists.
3. **CRM pipeline** — implement stages from raw lead through paid, onboarding sent, first value delivered, completed, testimonial/referral.
4. **Approval register** — all outbound WhatsApp/email/call/social drafts must sit in approvals before sending.
5. **RAG/KB governance** — approved service docs, pricing, FAQs, objections, support runbooks and guardrails in one shared knowledge base.
6. **Delivery templates** — scorecard, audit report, 30/60/90 roadmap, implementation backlog, proposal/SOW, testimonial request.
7. **Daily/weekly dashboard** — leads, approvals, outreach, replies, discovery calls, offers, deposits, first-value SLA, blockers.

## 30 / 60 / 90 plan

### First 30 days — make conversion and onboarding repeatable

Focus: turn existing assets into a controlled selling and onboarding machine.

- Finalize public/internal wording for the two main offers and two entry offers.
- Create one-page sales sheets for Local Growth Sprint and Cloud Advisory Sprint.
- Build Local and Advisory onboarding forms from the existing intake questions.
- Create first-value templates for both lanes.
- Wire CRM stages, approvals, inbox log, interactions and daily report into one operating rhythm.
- Expand RAG/voice/website KB with final approved offer copy, pricing ranges, FAQs, objection responses and escalation rules.
- Run only internal tests for website chatbot, WhatsApp response, voice SDR, CRM lead capture and founder alerts.
- Prepare outreach drafts for approval, but do not send without explicit approval.

30-day success gate:
- Offer ladder approved.
- Intake forms ready.
- First-value packs ready.
- CRM + approval SOP operational.
- Internal end-to-end test passes: lead → qualification → approval → discovery → deposit/onboarding simulation → first-value draft.

### Days 31–60 — run controlled pilots and collect proof

Focus: small, approved pilots with high learning density.

- Use approval-controlled outreach to a limited prospect batch only after human approval.
- Prioritize prospects with obvious pain: weak/no website, poor enquiry capture, no trust pages, rising cloud cost, cloud/security hiring signals.
- Book discovery calls with a short audit/demo objective, not a full consulting discussion.
- Convert at least one Local entry/main offer and one Advisory entry/main offer where possible.
- Deliver first-value pack within the SLA and document before/after evidence.
- Capture objections, pricing friction, service-fit issues and delivery bottlenecks.
- Improve offer copy, RAG KB, call scripts and proposal templates weekly.

60-day success gate:
- At least one paid or deposit-ready customer path validated, or clear blocker documented.
- First-value SLA achieved in real delivery or pilot delivery.
- Objection library updated from real conversations.
- One case-study/proof asset drafted if client permission allows.

### Days 61–90 — scale the winners safely

Focus: double down on the lane/offer with best traction.

- Increase approved prospect batches only after conversion metrics justify it.
- Productize delivery into checklists, templates and repeatable task boards.
- Add official WhatsApp Business API/WABA planning if volume or compliance requires it.
- Move from CSV-first CRM to database/dashboard only if lead volume demands it.
- Package 90-day retainers for customers who completed entry/main sprints.
- Build referral loop: completion review, testimonial request, referral ask, upsell roadmap.
- Review pricing and tighten guarantees based on real delivery effort.

90-day success gate:
- One clear flagship offer chosen for scale.
- Repeatable onboarding + first-value system proven.
- Minimum viable proof library exists.
- Approval-controlled outreach and compliance process remains intact.
- Next 90-day revenue target and channel plan approved.

## Approval gates

No external outreach, public posting, paid ads, prospect calls, customer WhatsApp sends, or client-facing pricing changes should happen without approval.

Required gates:
1. **Offer gate** — approve ladder, pricing, guarantees, exclusions.
2. **Message gate** — approve WhatsApp/email/call/social drafts before sending.
3. **Prospect gate** — approve target batch and contact channel.
4. **Spend gate** — approve any paid tools, ads, domains, APIs or subscriptions.
5. **Access gate** — approve any client/cloud/website/admin access request wording.
6. **Delivery gate** — approve proposal/SOW before sending.
7. **Proof gate** — approve use of client names, screenshots, testimonials or case studies.
