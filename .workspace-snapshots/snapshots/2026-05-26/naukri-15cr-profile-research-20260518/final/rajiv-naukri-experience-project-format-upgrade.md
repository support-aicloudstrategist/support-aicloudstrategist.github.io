## Formatting rule used
- Short executive opening line followed by bullet points.
- Each bullet starts with an action verb and shows scope, leadership, platform, governance, business impact, or risk reduction.
- Avoided paragraph blocks and unsupported exaggeration.
- AI/GPU/RAG wording kept evidence-safe: architecture, enablement, POC/readiness, governance — not unsupported production ownership.
