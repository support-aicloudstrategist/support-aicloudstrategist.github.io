# LinkedIn Draft 7 — Digital Bharat SMB stack

Status: INTERNAL DRAFT ONLY — not approved, not published.

The practical Digital Bharat stack for small businesses is not fancy.

It is:
- professional website
- public WhatsApp/call path
- enquiry form that actually works
- follow-up tracker
- owner dashboard
- privacy/consent basics
- clear service pages
- simple automation where it helps

The goal is not to impress technology people.

The goal is to help real business owners capture enquiries, respond faster, and operate with more trust.
