# LinkedIn Draft 3 — WhatsApp is the front desk

Status: INTERNAL DRAFT ONLY — not approved, not published.

For many Indian SMBs, WhatsApp is not “chat”.

It is the front desk.

Customers ask pricing, share requirements, request appointments, follow up, complain, and decide whether the business feels professional.

But without a system, WhatsApp becomes a black hole:
- no owner visibility
- no lead stage
- no pending callback list
- no weekly summary
- no clean handoff

The fix is not more software first.

The fix is a simple enquiry process that staff can actually follow.
