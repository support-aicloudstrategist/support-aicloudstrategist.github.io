## Best-fit segment

Most small labs do not need a heavy new system first. They need a clear view of whether patient enquiries, home collection requests, report-related questions and staff handovers are being captured, followed up and backed up safely across the tools they already use.

## Discovery questions

1. Where do most enquiries arrive today: phone, WhatsApp, website form, walk-in, aggregator, or doctor referral?
2. Who owns first response for home collection requests, and how is pending work tracked at day end?
3. How are report-download/report-delivery questions handled when patients cannot find or receive a report?
4. Can the owner/manager see missed callbacks and unresolved enquiries without asking each staff member?
5. What happens if the main phone, WhatsApp device, report portal login, or website admin access is unavailable for a day?
6. Are staff handovers documented when someone is absent or leaves?
7. Which workflow would have the fastest ROI: faster enquiry response, fewer repeated report calls, better callback tracking, backup/access cleanup, or a simple dashboard?
