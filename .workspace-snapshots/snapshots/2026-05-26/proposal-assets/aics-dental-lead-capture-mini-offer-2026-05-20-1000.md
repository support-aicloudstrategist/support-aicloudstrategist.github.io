## Discovery questions

1. Where do implant, aligner and cosmetic consultation enquiries most often arrive today: call, WhatsApp, website, Instagram/Facebook, Google profile or referral?
2. Who owns the first response when a high-value enquiry arrives, and how quickly is the first reply expected?
3. How are pending consultations, callbacks and no-shows tracked at day end?
4. What happens when the main phone/WhatsApp handler is unavailable or after-hours enquiries arrive?
5. Can the owner/clinic manager see unconverted high-value enquiries without asking individual staff?
6. Are treatment coordinators or front-desk staff using a shared board, spreadsheet, CRM or only chat/call history?
7. Which fix would create fastest ROI: faster first response, missed-call recovery, cleaner appointment reminders, owner visibility, or website/WhatsApp CTA cleanup?
