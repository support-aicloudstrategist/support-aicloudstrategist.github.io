## 4c. Partner agreement summary
This is a plain-English commercial summary for AICloudStrategist referral partners. It is not a substitute for a final signed agreement.

1. Referral commission: The partner receives 15% of the first month revenue actually received by AICloudStrategist from a referred client.
2. Payment timing: Commission is payable 30 days after AICloudStrategist receives the referred client’s first payment, provided the payment is not reversed, refunded, or disputed.
3. Written attribution required: The referral must be attributed in writing before or at the first sales discussion. Acceptable attribution includes email, CRM note, WhatsApp confirmation, or a partner referral form.
4. No exclusivity: The partner is free to work with other providers. AICloudStrategist is also free to work with other referral partners.
5. No minimums: There is no monthly referral target or minimum performance requirement.
6. Client relationship: AICloudStrategist owns delivery, pricing, scope, onboarding, and client communication after introduction unless otherwise agreed.
7. Compliance and honesty: Partners must not promise guaranteed results, legal advice, fabricated case studies, or discounts not approved by AICloudStrategist in writing.
8. Termination: Either side may terminate the referral arrangement with 30 days’ written notice. Valid referrals already accepted before termination remain eligible under the agreed terms.

AICloudStrategist is early-stage. The Founder Customer Program is in motion. Partner commission begins only after the referred client’s first payment is received by us.
