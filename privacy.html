Privacy Policy

How we handle website enquiries

This policy explains what we collect through this website, why we collect it, and how to contact us about your data.

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form or PolicyKart assessment, we may collect your name, business email, company name, website, service interest, assessment answers, readiness score, and the message you provide.

How We Use It

We use this information to respond to your enquiry, calculate and record a PolicyKart assessment when you request follow-up, schedule scoping calls, and improve our services.

Form and Analytics Providers

Website forms are processed through Formspree. Website analytics may be measured through privacy-focused analytics hosted at analytics.aicloudstrategist.com. These providers may process limited technical information needed to deliver their services.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers only when needed to operate this website, process enquiries, or comply with lawful requirements.

Retention

We keep enquiry and assessment information only as long as needed for business follow-up, record keeping, and legal or security purposes.

Your Choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete information you submitted through this website, subject to legal and operational retention needs.

Contact

For privacy requests, email contact@aicloudstrategist.com.

This policy is an operational website notice and is not legal advice.
