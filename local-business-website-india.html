Local business website guide for Indian SMBs.

What To Include

The website should answer the first sales call before it happens.

Most local SMB websites fail because they look generic, hide the contact path, or skip proof. Start with the information a serious buyer checks before calling.

Required pages

  • Home page with the main offer, city or service area, and clear call-to-action
  • Services or products page with practical details, not only broad category names
  • About page with business history, operating model, and owner credibility
  • Contact page with phone, WhatsApp, email, map or service area, and hours
  • Optional industry page for the highest-value customer segment

Trust signals

  • Real business photos, product photos, workshop photos, or team photos
  • GST, registration, certifications, associations, or years in business where relevant
  • Delivery areas, minimum order details, service process, and warranty basics
  • Privacy and consent notes for enquiry forms

Lead capture basics

  • Click-to-call on mobile
  • WhatsApp enquiry button with a useful pre-filled message
  • Short form that asks only for needed details
  • Owner notification flow and a daily lead tracking habit
SEO Basics

Make the business discoverable before spending on ads.

Use one page per real service category, write clear title tags, add a sitemap, connect Google Business Profile, and keep phone, address, and service area consistent across directories. Do not buy backlinks or stuff city names into unreadable copy.
