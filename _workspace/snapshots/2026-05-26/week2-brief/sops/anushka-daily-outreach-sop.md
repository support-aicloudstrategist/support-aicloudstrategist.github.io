# Anushka Daily Outreach SOP — AICloudStrategist

Audience: Anushka. Purpose: keep daily outreach consistent, approval-led, and founder-safe while moving qualified owner-led clinics/labs from prospect to audit conversation.

## Operating rules

- Do not send cold WhatsApp to more than 5 new prospects/day.
- No bulk blasting. Every message must match the prospect category and approved template.
- Founder approval is required before sending any audit PDF, new WhatsApp creative, new email sequence, or non-standard promise.
- Log every touch in the CRM tracker the same day.
- If a prospect asks to stop, log `declined/opt-out` and do not follow up.

## Monday — pipeline setup

Targets:
- 5 cold WhatsApp messages from approved templates.
- 15 manual LinkedIn DMs.
- 1 LinkedIn post.
- 3-5 call follow-ups from prior warm/queued prospects.

Checklist:
1. Pick today’s 5 highest-fit queued prospects.
2. Confirm owner name, business name, city, and best available channel.
3. Send only approved WhatsApp/email/DM copy.
4. Call warm prospects only if a number is verified and calling is approved.
5. Update CRM before end of day.

## Tuesday — audit booking push

Targets:
- 5 cold WhatsApp messages.
- 15 LinkedIn DMs.
- 1 LinkedIn post.
- 3-5 call follow-ups.

Focus: move interested replies toward free Lost-Lead Audit submission or manual audit intake.

If prospect says yes:
- Share the free audit link or collect only required business details.
- Mark status `interested`.
- Create/confirm lead JSON for audit workflow.
- Generate audit only after the lead data is complete.

## Wednesday — trust/DPDP angle

Targets remain the same.

Focus: use DPDP/trust readiness language for clinics/labs where the website has forms, patient enquiry capture, or missing privacy signals.

Do not scare prospects. Keep tone practical: “small trust gaps can reduce enquiries.”

## Thursday — follow-up day

Targets:
- 5 cold WhatsApp messages maximum.
- 15 LinkedIn DMs.
- 1 LinkedIn post.
- 3-5 call follow-ups.
- Clear due follow-ups Day +1/+3/+7.

Use sequence:
- Day +1: gentle “did the audit reach you?”
- Day +3: DPDP Sprint / trust cleanup offer based on audit findings.
- Day +7: final value reminder and ask for thoughts even if not a fit.

Every follow-up requires the generated approval pack before send.

## Friday — close loops and report

Targets remain the same, but prioritise:
- Replies pending action.
- Audits delivered but not acknowledged.
- Interested prospects needing next-step proposal.
- Declines/ghosts to close cleanly.

End-of-day report:
- Dials made.
- Conversations.
- Audits sent.
- Replies received.
- Audits delivered.
- Follow-ups due next week.
- Blockers.

## Weekend — 1-hour review

Spend one hour only:
1. Review which messages got replies.
2. Pick next week’s top 25 prospects.
3. Draft one LinkedIn post idea for each weekday.
4. Check whether any template needs founder review.
5. Confirm Monday’s first-touch list.

## Daily CRM metrics

Log these fields daily:
- `dials_made`
- `conversations`
- `audits_sent`
- `replies_received`
- `audits_delivered`
- `followups_due`
- `next_action`
- `owner`
- `status`

## Decision tree

Prospect says yes:
- Mark `interested`.
- Confirm business name, website, WhatsApp, email, vertical.
- Create audit lead record.
- Generate audit PDF.
- Prepare founder approval pack.
- Send only after approval.

Prospect ghosts:
- If audit delivered, use Day +1/+3/+7 follow-up packs.
- If cold outreach only, one polite follow-up only unless they re-engage.
- Mark status based on last touch and next due date.

Prospect declines:
- Reply politely if appropriate.
- Log `declined` or `not interested`.
- Close without argument.

Prospect asks pricing:
- Do not improvise discounts.
- Offer audit-first review or share approved package positioning.
- Escalate custom pricing to founder approval.

## 15-minute daily checklist

Tick these before closing the day:

- Selected today’s prospects.
- Verified owner/business/channel.
- Sent within daily limits.
- Logged every touch.
- Created approval packs where needed.
- Checked replies.
- Updated follow-up due dates.
