## Recommended First 30-Day Execution Sequence

### Week 1: Build assets and activate warm channels
- Create one-page partner/referral brief.
- Prepare CA, web agency, and IT vendor partner sheets.
- Optimize Google Business Profile basics.
- Draft LinkedIn content and DM templates for approval, but do not send without approval.

### Week 2: Partner discovery
- Map 25 CAs/accountants, 25 web agencies, and 25 IT/network vendors.
- Prioritize existing relationships and local credibility first.
- Schedule partner conversations only after approval.
- Create a simple CRM sheet with source, fit, status, next action, and consent notes.

### Week 3: Authority channels
- Approach MSME associations and chambers for educational sessions.
- Finalize a 20-30 minute webinar/clinic deck.
- Publish or schedule approved educational LinkedIn posts.
- Add marketplace/listing profiles with consistent positioning.

### Week 4: Run first clinic and measure
- Host one webinar/clinic or association session if approved.
- Track lead source, lead quality, problem type, budget signal, and next step.
- Double down on the top 2 channels by qualified lead rate, not raw volume.

## Practical Channel Ranking for Early ROI

1. CAs/accountants  
2. Referral partners  
3. IT/network vendors  
4. Web agencies  
5. MSME/industrial associations  
6. Local chambers  
7. Webinars/clinics  
8. Google Business Profile/local SEO  
9. LinkedIn content/approved DM  
10. Marketplaces/listing sites

## Notes

- Best near-term strategy: partner-led acquisition plus educational authority building.
- Avoid mass outreach. Use consent, referrals, opt-ins, and approved messaging.
- Track lead quality weekly; volume alone can be misleading for consulting services.
