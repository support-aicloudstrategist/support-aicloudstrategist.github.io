## Discovery call agenda — 20 minutes

1. **Current flow map — 5 min**  
   Where enquiries arrive: Google profile, website, phone, WhatsApp, referrals, existing patients.

2. **Leakage points — 5 min**  
   Missed calls, delayed WhatsApp replies, report-status repeat queries, manual registers, unassigned follow-ups.

3. **Owner visibility — 5 min**  
   How the owner/manager currently knows what is pending, who followed up, and what was lost.

4. **Next-step recommendation — 5 min**  
   Decide whether they need a simple follow-up board, enquiry/report request workflow, website/Google profile fix, or a broader cloud readiness cleanup.

## Discovery questions

- Which channels bring the most new enquiries today?
- Who checks missed calls and WhatsApp messages when the front desk is busy?
- How are report/status requests tracked after sample collection?
- Do you have a daily list of pending callbacks or pending patient requests?
- What happens if the person handling enquiries is absent?
- Is there a website/contact form/Google Business Profile, and who receives those leads?
- What would be most valuable: faster response, fewer missed enquiries, better owner visibility, or cleaner follow-up tracking?
