# LinkedIn Draft 4 — A website is not enough

Status: INTERNAL DRAFT ONLY — not approved, not published.

A website alone does not create growth.

A useful SMB digital system needs three layers:

1. Presence
Can customers understand and contact you quickly?

2. Automation
Are enquiries captured, assigned, followed up, and reported?

3. Trust
Are privacy, consent, proof, and process hygiene visible?

Many businesses stop at layer 1.

The opportunity is to build all three without making the owner learn complicated tools.
