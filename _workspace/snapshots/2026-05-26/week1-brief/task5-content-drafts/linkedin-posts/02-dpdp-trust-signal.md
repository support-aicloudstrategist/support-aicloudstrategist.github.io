# LinkedIn Draft 2 — DPDP readiness as trust signal

Status: INTERNAL DRAFT ONLY — not approved, not published.

For small healthcare businesses, privacy readiness is not only a compliance topic.

It is a trust signal.

A patient sharing name, phone number, reports, appointment details, or medical context wants confidence that the business handles data responsibly.

Simple first steps:
- visible privacy note
- consent/helper text near forms
- clear WhatsApp enquiry handling
- staff access discipline
- vendor/tool register
- retention basics

This is not legal advice. It is operational hygiene that every serious business should start building.
