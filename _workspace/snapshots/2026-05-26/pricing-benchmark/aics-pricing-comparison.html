Pricing verdict

Launch Desk: low-to-mid. Lead Recovery: mid-market. AI Sales Desk: mid-to-high for Indian SMB SaaS, but mid for managed implementation. Scale: premium.

Sales message

Do not compare AICS with only WhatsApp SaaS. Compare it with website + WhatsApp + AI chatbot + CRM + lead recovery + monthly optimization together.

Sources checked: AiSensy, WATI, Gallabox, Interakt, QuickReply.ai, Respond.io, Tidio, Hostinger, LogoX, WebGuru/Webpulse-style agencies. Public pricing may change; WhatsApp/Meta/API/GST/extra user charges are commonly separate.
