## 4) Leadership discovery prompts

Ask only what is needed; keep the call practical and time-boxed.

1. Where do most enquiries come from today: Google, walk-ins, phone, WhatsApp, website, referrals, or campaigns?
2. What happens when a call or WhatsApp enquiry is missed?
3. Which step creates the most staff follow-up: appointment booking, billing, report delivery, review requests, or repeat visits?
4. Which systems do not talk to each other today?
5. Which reports or operational numbers does leadership wish they could see daily?
6. What has broken or slowed down in the last 90 days: PC/server, internet, software, backup, staff handover, vendor support?
7. When was the last backup restore test performed? If unknown, mark as a risk without requesting system access.
8. What is the one improvement that would save the most time or reduce the most leakage in the next 30 days?
