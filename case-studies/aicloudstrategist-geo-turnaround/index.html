Real self-case study

AICloudStrategist GEO turnaround: 37/100 → 67/100.

This is a real public self-case study based on our own website audit and re-audit. It is not a client case study and does not claim customer revenue, bookings, reviews, or savings.

Situation

The website had strong service intent but weak machine readability. The baseline GEO audit found AI crawlers blocked, no llms.txt, no structured data, no FAQ/Article/Service schema, and weak extractable entity signals.

What changed

  • Unblocked major AI crawlers in robots.txt.
  • Removed the conflicting AI training/crawler signal.
  • Published a clear llms.txt with services, contact details, positioning, and claim boundaries.
  • Added Organization, WebSite, WebPage, Person, ContactPoint, and Breadcrumb schema.
  • Added FAQPage, Article, Service, and Offer schema where relevant.
  • Corrected Cloudflare Managed robots settings that were overriding crawler access.

Measured result

The re-audit scored AICloudStrategist at 67/100, up from 37/100. Technical GEO and schema moved sharply because AI systems could now read, classify, and cite the site more reliably.

Boundary: this is internal/public self-proof, not a client success story. It should not be represented as customer revenue, legal compliance, patient bookings, or guaranteed SEO/GEO ranking.

Next improvement target

The audit still highlighted proof, homepage depth, and off-site brand authority. That is why the current roadmap adds honest proof pages, stronger homepage extraction text, populated profiles, real reviews only when earned, and third-party corroboration over time.
