Free Checklist

SMB website checklist for Indian businesses.

Use this before building or fixing a website for a factory, trader, workshop, furniture business, distributor, or owner-led service firm.

Check before launchTrustLead captureSEO basicsOperations handoff

1. Business Clarity

Homepage says what you sell, who you serve, where you operate, and how to contact you. Avoid generic agency-style language.

2. Minimum Pages

Home, services or products, about, contact, privacy policy, and one segment page if customers search by industry or location.

3. Lead Capture

Every page should have click-to-call, WhatsApp, and an enquiry form. The owner or assigned staff should receive form submissions immediately.

4. Trust Signals

Add legal business name, service area, photos or catalogue where available, clear contact details, founder or team note, and realistic delivery/service claims.

5. SEO Basics

Each page needs a descriptive title, meta description, clean URL, mobile-friendly layout, sitemap, robots file, and local keywords in natural language.

6. Google Business Profile Inputs

Prepare business category, address or service area, phone, website link, opening hours, photos, services, and first update post.

7. Follow-Up Process

Decide who calls new leads, within what time, where status is tracked, and what message is sent if the customer does not answer.
