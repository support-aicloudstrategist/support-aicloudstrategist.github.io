Page Plan

Show enough detail for a serious B2B buyer to enquire.

Factory and supplier websites need more specificity than a generic local service page. Buyers want capability, capacity, material, process, location, and contact confidence.

Core pages

  • Home page with product categories and industry focus
  • Capabilities page covering machinery, process, material, size range, or service capacity
  • Products or services page with practical examples and enquiry prompts
  • About page with business history, location, certifications, and operating strengths
  • Contact page with phone, WhatsApp, email, map, and quote request form

Proof to collect

  • Workshop, plant, machine, stock, product, packaging, and dispatch photos
  • Industries served, regions served, and minimum order or project fit
  • Quality checks, warranty terms, sample policy, or delivery expectations
  • Customer names only when approved for public use

Quote form fields

  • Product or service needed
  • Material, size, quantity, drawing, photo, or reference
  • Delivery location and timeline
  • Buyer company, phone, and email
Next Step

Connect enquiries to follow-up.

Factory enquiries often need clarification, quote preparation, and multiple callbacks. A simple tracker should record enquiry source, requirement, quote date, owner, and next follow-up.
