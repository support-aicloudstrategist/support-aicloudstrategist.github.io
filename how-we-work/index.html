How it works

A simple process designed for busy business owners.

1

Free Business Growth Review

You share your website, contact path, and business category. We review your public presence, enquiry journey, follow-up gaps, trust signals, and promotion opportunities, then send practical next steps. Time needed: delivered within 48 working hours.

2

Discovery call (optional, 15 min)

The call is optional because the review should make sense on its own. If you want help deciding what to fix first, we walk through the findings, explain the simplest next step, and recommend the simplest setup that can solve the problem. Time needed: 15 minutes after you read the review.

3

Build & launch (2-4 weeks)

Once there is approval before launch, we build the agreed assets: website pages, enquiry forms, WhatsApp or preferred-channel templates, simple tracking, trust basics, creatives, and automation where included. You get progress updates and clear approval points so the work does not become confusing. Time needed: around 2 weeks for setup work or 4 weeks for a full build & launch.

4

Sign-off and handover

Nothing important goes live without approval. We test the forms, links, follow-up flow, trust pages, and handover notes, then give you a simple record of what was built and how to use it. Time needed: 1 to 2 working days after the final review.

5

Optional monthly support

If you continue with optional monthly support, we monitor the growth system, suggest ongoing improvement, and prepare a monthly report in plain language. The focus is clearer customer follow-up, cleaner records, stronger trust signals, and better promotion. Time needed: ongoing month to month.
