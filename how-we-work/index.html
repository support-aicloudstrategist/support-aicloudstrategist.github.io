How it works

A simple process designed for busy business owners.

1

Free Business Growth Review

You share your website, contact path, and business category. We review your public presence, enquiry journey, follow-up gaps, trust signals, and promotion opportunities, then send practical next steps. Typical timeline: delivered within 48 working hours.

2

Discovery call (optional, 15 min)

The call is optional because the review should make sense on its own. If you want help deciding what to fix first, we walk through the findings, explain the simplest next step, and recommend the smallest service sprint that can solve the problem. Typical timeline: 15 minutes after you read the review.

3

Sprint or Setup (2-4 weeks)

Once the scope is approved, we build the agreed assets: website pages, enquiry forms, WhatsApp or preferred-channel templates, simple tracking, trust basics, creatives, and automation where included. You get progress updates and clear approval points so the work does not become confusing. Typical timeline: 2 weeks for a sprint or 4 weeks for a full setup.

4

Sign-off and handover

Nothing important goes live without approval. We test the forms, links, follow-up flow, trust pages, and handover notes, then give you a simple record of what was built and how to use it. Typical timeline: 1 to 2 working days after the final review.

5

Monthly retainer (ongoing)

If you continue on a retainer, we monitor the growth system, suggest improvements, and prepare a monthly report in plain language. The focus is continuous improvement: fewer missed enquiries, clearer customer follow-up, cleaner records, stronger trust signals, and better promotion. Typical timeline: ongoing month to month.
