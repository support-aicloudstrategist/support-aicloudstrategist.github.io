How we work

A simple process designed for busy business owners.

1

Free Lost-Lead Audit (48 hours)

You fill the form with your website, WhatsApp number, and business category. We review your public website, Google Business Profile signals, enquiry path, WhatsApp readiness, and visible DPDP exposure, then send a practical PDF with the biggest leaks and fixes. Typical timeline: delivered within 48 working hours.

2

Discovery call (optional, 15 min)

The call is optional because the audit should make sense on its own. If you want help deciding what to fix first, we walk through the findings, explain the simplest next step, and recommend the smallest package that can solve the problem. Typical timeline: 15 minutes after you read the audit.

3

Sprint or Setup (2-4 weeks)

Once the scope is approved, we build the agreed assets: DPDP basics, website changes, lead forms, WhatsApp templates, simple CRM tracking, and automation where included. You get weekly progress updates and clear approval points so the work does not become confusing. Typical timeline: 2 weeks for a sprint or 4 weeks for a full setup.

4

Sign-off and handover

Nothing important goes live without your approval. We test the forms, links, WhatsApp flow, privacy pages, and handover notes, then give you a simple record of what was built and how to use it. Typical timeline: 1 to 2 working days after the final review.

5

Monthly retainer (ongoing)

If you continue on a retainer, we monitor the lead recovery system, suggest improvements, and prepare a monthly report in plain language. The focus is continuous improvement: fewer missed enquiries, clearer customer follow-up, cleaner records, and better readiness for privacy questions. Typical timeline: ongoing month to month.
