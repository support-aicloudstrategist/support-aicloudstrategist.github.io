# WhatsApp Consent Flow Template

1. Form helper: “By submitting, you agree that [BUSINESS] may contact you on phone, email or WhatsApp about this enquiry.”
2. First WhatsApp reply: “Thanks for contacting [BUSINESS]. We will use this chat only for your enquiry, appointment coordination and requested follow-up. Reply STOP if you do not want further follow-up.”
3. Reminder: “Your appointment/test booking is scheduled for [DATE/TIME]. Reply 1 to confirm or 2 to reschedule.”
4. Promotional opt-in, separate: “Would you like to receive occasional offers/health camp updates on WhatsApp? Reply YES to opt in.”

Do not ask for sensitive medical details unless the clinic/lab has approved that workflow.
