## Page 7 — Estimated leads lost per month

Use the formula, do not fabricate inputs:

`Estimated leads lost/month = monthly enquiries × estimated leakage rate`

Where:
- **monthly enquiries** = client-stated count OR public proxy clearly labelled as an estimate
- **estimated leakage rate** = percentage justified from visible friction and client process input
- **revenue impact** = leads lost × estimated booking rate × average ticket value

If inputs are unavailable, write `UNKNOWN` and show what data is needed.
